## DataX Lab

DataX Lab, located at the Advanced Engineering Building (AEB) at UNLV, was
established in 2015. We develop novel and efficient algorithms to solve
challenging computational problems in bioinformatics and big data analytics.

### Research Focus
- Interpretable and Integrative Deep Learning
- Next Generation Sequencing
- Multi-omics Data Analysis
- Clinical and Translational Research
- Computer Vision
